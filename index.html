About Smart Care Optic

Your Trusted Vision Partner

Founded in 2010, Smart Care Optic has grown from a small family-run optical shop into one of the most trusted names in eyewear. Our mission is simple: help you see clearly and look great doing it.

We believe that every pair of glasses should be a perfect blend of function and fashion. Our team of certified optometrists and style consultants work together to ensure you leave our store with confidence.
